WOD (Work Out Of The Day)

Description: 
An application where people can share their WOD, post a WOD that they programed, and see their WODS.

MVP Features: 
1. User can post a WOD that they did 
2. User can post a WOD that they programed and would like to do in the future
3. User can see or get a list of their programed WODs and past WODs
4. User can move a WOD from programed list to their done list
